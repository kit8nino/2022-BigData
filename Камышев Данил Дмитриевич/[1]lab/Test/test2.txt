Team Fractal Alligator is a developer of video games.
Team Fractal Alligator has been active on the market since before 2015.
Its first and so far only project was game Hacknet.
In publishing its games, Team Fractal Alligator collaborates with Surprise Attack Games / Fellow Traveller. The developer's most recent work is Hacknet. Production release occurred on 13 August 2015.
